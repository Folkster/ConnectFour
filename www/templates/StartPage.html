Connect Four

Welcome to Connect Four - a two-dimensional, not very exciting game.

Connect Four has remained a timeless game over the years for very good reason. The game is fun and approachable, which makes it accessible for people of all ages. If you have never played Connect Four, then you are in for a treat. You will have a blast playing this game with your friends or our AI-opponent.

By clicking on the play-button a new game against an AI-opponent or a friend is initiated. Feel free to read up on the rules before you play
